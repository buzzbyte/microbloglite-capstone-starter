WELCOME TO PROJECT RIBBIT

Who are We

Ribbit was created by 4 amazing coders. We created Ribbit with the aim of fostering a vibrant online community dedicated to sharing and discussing topics related to personal growth, self-improvement, and well-being. Our primary motivation was to create a space where individuals from all walks of life could come together to exchange ideas, experiences, and insights, ultimately inspiring and empowering one another.

How to Use the Site

To use Ribbit all you need to do is sign up. Once you sign up for Ribbit you have full control of the profile. From customizing your profile picture to editing your bio. To simply post on Ribbit, all you must do is fill the message you in the box and hit Ribble and all your friends and see what you post. To view only your own post, you can stay on your own profile page. To view your and your friends post you can click on the post page.
